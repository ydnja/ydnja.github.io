How can drones help in conservation?

Drones can be used in collecting data about species and their habitats. This is particularly important, because the main reason of death in wildlife workers in US is due to aircraft collisions and technical issues.
In order for data to be collected the recording tool should be up in the air, in one spot for a period of time, and that's exactly what drones can do!
